Once upon a time, in a land filled with sparkling rivers and candy-colored trees, lived a little dragon named Sparky.

Sparky was not like the other dragons.

He couldn't breathe fire. Instead, every time he tried, he sneezed out a shower of shimmering, colorful bubbles!

The other dragons would laugh, but Sparky was sad. He wanted to be a real dragon.

One day, a big, grumpy bear came to the village. All the dragons tried to scare him away with their fire, but the bear just growled.

Then, little Sparky stepped forward. He took a deep breath, and... ACHOO!

A giant wave of bubbles floated towards the bear. They tickled his nose and made him giggle.

The grumpy bear wasn't grumpy anymore! He became friends with all the dragons, and Sparky learned that his special bubbles were a gift.
